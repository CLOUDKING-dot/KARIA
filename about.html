About Karia

Karia's mission is to empower communities to thrive sustainably. We believe that everyone in cities should have access to fast, friendly delivery services at convenience. We are a venture-backed startup, currently expanding across Kampala City, aiming to become the major logistics agency.

Our Drivers Community

Our community of Karia couriers receive extensive training to make them the safest and most professional deliveries on the streets. When drivers join the Karia community, they see a significant increase in their business, providing impact to themselves, their families, and the wider community.

Our Values

Community

We started as a small group of couriers and entrepreneurs. We care about our community of staff, drivers, agents, and customers.

Sustainability

Our courier fleet powers fast friendly deliveries, saving businesses and individuals.

Innovation

We challenge the status quo with smart solutions that make logistics reliable and faster.

Trust

We expect customers to trust our drivers and agents, so we build trust in everything we do.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest standard and deliver what we promise.

Who is Backing Us

We are a venture-funded company, backed by leaders in sustainability and tech, making a huge impact across Africa.
