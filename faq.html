Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Karia’s eco-friendly delivery services. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to contact our support team!

FAQ

How does Karia ensure eco-friendly deliveries?

We use an electric vehicle fleet to minimize carbon emissions, ensuring sustainable logistics across Africa.

How can I track my delivery?

Once your order is placed, you’ll receive real-time updates in the Karia app, including your driver’s name and estimated arrival time.

What areas does Karia operate in?

We’re currently expanding across Africa. Check the app for availability in your city!

Can I pay with cash?

Yes, we offer cash and digital payment options. Choose your preferred method in the app.

How do I become a Karia driver?

Visit our "Join Us" page, select "Driver," and follow the application process. We provide training and support!

What happens if my package is delayed?

Contact our support team via the app or email support@karia.com. We’ll resolve it promptly.

Is there a limit to package size?

Our EVs can handle most standard packages. For oversized items, contact support for assistance.

How do I contact support?

Reach us at support@karia.com, call +1-800-BLUEEV, or use the in-app chat feature.
